My Trip to TTD

Recently I visited Tirumala Tirupathi Devasthanam, The journey filled my heart with happiness and connecting deeper to my spiritual self.
finally i observe it is not just a place it is literally "Heaven".

Tips for visiters

  • Try to go On steps route for nature lovers.
  • Don't carry Alcoholic items.
  • Use only Dustbins.
  • Prefer Traditional Dressing.
  • Try to Explore other local atractions like Akasa Ganga.
