Kanyakumari is a place which is situated in Tamilnadu,which is famous for Sunrise.

Kanyakumari, also known as Cape Comorin, is a town in Tamil Nadu, India, situated at the southernmost tip of mainland India. It's famous for the confluence of the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean. The town is also known for its beautiful sunrise and sunset views, as well as its temples, beaches, and historical landmarks like the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.
